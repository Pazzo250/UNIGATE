Scholarship Application Tips

1

Start Early

Begin your scholarship search 12-18 months before your intended start date. Many scholarships have early deadlines.

2

Read Requirements Carefully

3

Prepare Strong Essays

Take time to write compelling personal statements that highlight your unique story and aspirations.

4

Gather References

Secure strong letters of recommendation from teachers, mentors, or employers who know you well.

5

Apply to Multiple Scholarships

Don't put all your eggs in one basket. Apply to several scholarships that match your profile.

6

Follow Up

After applying, follow up politely if you haven't heard back within the expected timeframe.
