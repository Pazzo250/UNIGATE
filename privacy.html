Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how UNIGATE collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Educational background and academic records
  • Application preferences and university choices
  • Payment information for premium services
  • Communications with our support team

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Device information and screen resolution
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your university applications
  • Provide personalized recommendations and support
  • Communicate with you about your applications
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Send you relevant updates and notifications
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Universities: To process your applications to selected institutions
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate our platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to sharing

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure data storage with regular backups
  • Access controls and employee training
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Multi-factor authentication for staff access

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our platform
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to show relevant advertisements

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud

Application data is typically retained for 7 years after your last activity.

8. International Data Transfers

As a global platform serving African students, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • Prominent notice on our platform
  • Update of the "Last updated" date above

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
